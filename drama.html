Drama Competition

Express stories. Live the characters. Own the stage 🎭

About Event

The Drama Competition is a creative platform where participants bring stories to life through acting, dialogue, and expressions. Teams can perform skits, mime, or theatrical plays based on social, emotional, or humorous themes. Participants are judged on acting skills, script quality, coordination, and stage presence. This event encourages creativity, storytelling, and teamwork while entertaining the audience.

Drama Categories

Stage Play

Full-length dramatic performances with strong storytelling.

Mime

Express emotions and stories without using words.

Street Play

Social awareness dramas performed in an engaging style.

Comedy Skit

Short humorous performances to entertain the audience.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Time limit: 5–10 minutes
  • Team participation only
  • No offensive or inappropriate content
  • Props must be arranged by participants

Judging Criteria

  • Acting & Expression – 30%
  • Story & Creativity – 25%
  • Coordination – 25%
  • Overall Impact – 20%
