Talentina’26 Fashion Show is a vibrant celebration of style, creativity, and self-expression. This event brings together confident young talents who are ready to showcase their fashion sense, stage presence, and unique personalities on the runway. It is a platform where elegance meets innovation, and every participant gets the opportunity to shine with grace and confidence.

Legacy of Talentina

Who We Are

Talentina has grown into one of Asia’s largest cultural festivals, a symbol of tradition, creativity, and unity, bringing together people through music, art, and experiences that transcend boundaries.

  • Hosting world-class performances & global artists
  • A platform for students to innovate and create
  • Over 50 years of cultural legacy and excellence

Event Venue

Talentina'26 will be held at the prestigious Grand Ballroom of the City Convention Center.

Event Time

The event will take place on the 15th of May, 2026, from 6:00 PM to 10:00 PM.

Event Rules

Participants must adhere to the following rules to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone:

  • Participants must be between the ages of 18 and 30.
  • All outfits must be original and created by the participants.
  • Participants must register before the deadline of April 30, 2026.
  • Each participant is allowed to bring one assistant for backstage support.
  • Participants must attend the rehearsal on May 10, 2026.

CATAGORIES

Participants can choose from the following categories to showcase their talents:

Traditional Wear

Displays seasonal women's fashion, blending creativity with wearability for consumers and stylists.

Formal Runway

Traditional parades with models walking linear paths, common at fashion weeks.

Charity/Informal

Less structured events for fundraising or casual presentations.

Haute Couture

Features handcrafted, custom-made luxury garments for elite clients; emphasizes artistry over mass production.

REWARDS & PRIZES

With cash prizes, trophies, gift vouchers, and merit certificates as grand rewards, the event offers a life-changing platform—not just for category winners celebrated in spectacular prize distributions, but for emerging talents who secure recognition, build portfolios, and step toward careers in modeling, design, and fashion entertainment
