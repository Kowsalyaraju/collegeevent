Legacy of Talentina

Who We Are

Talentina has grown into one of Asia’s largest cultural festivals, a symbol of tradition, creativity, and unity, bringing together people through music, art, and experiences that transcend boundaries.

  • Hosting world-class performances & global artists
  • A platform for students to innovate and create
  • Over 50 years of cultural legacy and excellence

Event Rules

Participants must adhere to the following rules to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone:

CATAGORIES

Participants can choose from the following categories to showcase their talents:

Western Rock/Pop

Covers or originals in guitar-driven styles with drums and vocals.

Indian Fusion

Blends Carnatic/Hindustani rhythms with modern instruments.

Acoustic Bands

Unplugged sets focusing on vocals, guitar, and minimal percussion.

Novice Bands

Beginner groups emphasizing fun over complexity with catchy tunes
