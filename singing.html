About Event

The Singing Competition is a platform for talented vocalists to showcase their passion and musical skills. Participants can perform in various genres such as classical, melody, pop, and western. Performers can participate individually or as a group and express emotions through their voice and performance. Judges will evaluate based on voice quality, pitch accuracy, expression, and stage presence. This event promises a soulful and entertaining experience for both performers and audience.

Singing Categories

Classical

Traditional singing styles focusing on ragas and expressions.

Melody

Soft and emotional songs with smooth vocals.

Western

Pop, jazz, and contemporary singing styles.

Rap

Fast-paced rhythmic singing with attitude and flow.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Time limit: 3–5 minutes
  • Solo and group performances allowed
  • No lip-sync allowed
  • Karaoke track must be submitted in advance

Judging Criteria

  • Voice Quality – 30%
  • Pitch & Timing – 25%
  • Expression & Feel – 25%
  • Stage Presence – 20%
