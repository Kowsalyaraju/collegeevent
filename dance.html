Dance Competition

Feel the rhythm. Express the emotion. Own the stage

About Event

Dance Competition is a fun and energetic event where dancers show their talent in styles like classical, folk, freestyle, and contemporary. Participants can perform solo or in groups and showcase creativity and choreography. Judges will score based on creativity, coordination, stage presence, and overall performance. Winners will get prizes and recognition. Overall, it’s an exciting platform for dancers and a great entertainment experience for the audience.

Dance Categories

Classical

Traditional styles like Bharatanatyam focusing on grace and expressions.

Western

Modern dance forms like freestyle, jazz, and contemporary.

Hip-Hop

Energetic street-style dance with powerful movements.

Freestyle

Creative dance with no limits—express your own style.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Time limit: 3–5 minutes
  • Solo and group performances allowed
  • No inappropriate content
  • Music must be submitted prior to the event

Judging Criteria

  • Choreography & Creativity – 30%
  • Synchronization & Timing – 25%
  • Expressions & Stage Presence – 25%
  • Overall Impact – 20%
